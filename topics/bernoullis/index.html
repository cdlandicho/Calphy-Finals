Bernoulli's Principle

Understanding Fluid Dynamics and Pressure

Bernoulli's Principle states that as the velocity of a fluid increases, its pressure decreases. This principle explains airplane lift, fluid flow in pipes, and blood circulation.

Key Concepts

  • Pressure Energy: Stored due to fluid pressure
  • Kinetic Energy: Energy from fluid motion (½ρv²)
  • Potential Energy: Energy due to elevation (ρgh)
  • Conservation: Total energy remains constant along flow

Bernoulli's Equation

P + ½ρv² + ρgh = constant
